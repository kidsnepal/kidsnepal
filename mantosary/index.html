About Us

Discover the Montessori Difference

Learn about our child-centered approach to education

🎯

Montessori Method

We follow the proven Montessori methodology that emphasizes self-directed activity, hands-on learning, and collaborative play. Our trained educators guide each child through individualized learning paths.

🌍

Our Location

Located in the heart of Pokhara at Dharapani, Parsyang-5, our school provides a safe, nurturing environment where children can learn and grow. Pokhara's beautiful surroundings enhance the learning experience.

💡

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide holistic development for every child, fostering curiosity, creativity, and confidence. We prepare children for lifelong learning through engaging activities and a supportive environment.
